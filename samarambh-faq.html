01 How many guests can Samarambh accommodate?

02 Is outside catering allowed? Can we bring our own caterer?

Outside catering is not permitted. All food must be arranged through Samarambh's in-house kitchen, which allows us to maintain consistent quality and hygiene across every event.

Our kitchen offers both Veg and Non-Veg menus, with each per-plate package covering starters, mains, and dessert. Menus can be customised based on your event type and preferences — speak to our team to discuss options.

Outside caterers not permitted Veg menus available Non-Veg menus available Per-plate pricing varies by event & date
03 Is outside alcohol permitted? Do you have a bar?

Yes — outside licensed alcohol is welcome at Samarambh. The venue does not stock or supply alcohol directly, but you are free to bring your own, provided it is legally licensed.

If you need assistance, Samarambh can arrange a liquor licence on your behalf for a charge of ₹20,000. This covers the necessary permits so your celebration goes smoothly without any compliance concerns.

Alcohol not stocked at venue Licensed outside alcohol permitted Liquor licence arranged by venue — ₹20,000
04 What are the venue timings? Are late-night events allowed?

Samarambh operates on two daily slots, allowing you to book either a daytime or an evening celebration:

Lunch slot — 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM Dinner slot — 6:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight)

The dinner slot runs until midnight, which accommodates most evening wedding receptions and sangeet programmes comfortably. Any extensions beyond the standard slot are subject to venue terms and conditions — speak with our team at the time of booking.

05 Is a DJ or live music allowed? What AV is available?

Yes, music and entertainment are very much a part of the Samarambh experience. The venue provides a basic sound system and stage as part of the setup, along with full power backup so the celebration never misses a beat.

DJ packages start from ₹8,000 and can be arranged through the venue. Projectors and microphones are also available at additional charges. For Homa or fire rituals, Havan/Homa is permitted on the premises.

Stage & sound system included Full power backup Homa / fire pooja permitted DJ from ₹8,000 Projector & mic — additional charges
06 Is parking available? How do guests get to the venue?

Samarambh offers dedicated on-site parking for up to 75 vehicles, with a professional valet service available on event days. The venue sits directly on Ghodbunder Road, one of Thane's main arterial roads, making it easy to find and accessible from all directions.

Parking for 75 vehicles Valet parking available On Ghodbunder Road, Thane West Thane Bus Stop & Metro nearby ~56 km from Mumbai Airport
07 Do you have guest rooms or accommodation on-site?

Yes. Samarambh has 13 guest rooms on the premises — a meaningful convenience for out-of-town family and the bridal or groom's party who need to be nearby before or after the event.

Having accommodation within the venue removes the stress of coordinating separate hotels for close family, and makes early morning events (like Haldi or Mehendi) far more manageable. Please check availability with our team when enquiring about your event date.

13 guest rooms on-site Availability based on event date

10 How do I book? What is the advance payment and cancellation policy?

Booking Samarambh is straightforward. Begin by reaching out via phone, WhatsApp, or the inquiry form to confirm your event date and venue availability. Once details are agreed, a 50% advance secures your booking. The remaining balance is due 15 days before the event date.

Please note that bookings are non-cancellable and non-refundable, so we recommend confirming all details carefully before payment. Standard GST is applicable on the total booking amount.

50% advance confirms booking Balance due 15 days before event Non-cancellable & non-refundable Standard GST applicable
