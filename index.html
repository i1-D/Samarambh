Everything You
Need to Know

Still have questions? Our team is available 7 days a week to guide you through every detail.

How many guests can Samarambh accommodate? +

Samarambh has four distinct spaces, each suited to a different scale of celebration — so whether you're hosting an intimate gathering or a grand shaadi, there's a venue for you.

  • Lawn 1 — 700 seated / 1,000 floating
  • Lawn 2 — 1,500 seated / 2,000 floating
  • Jalsa — 650 seated / 1,000 floating
  • Banquet Hall — 300 seated / 500 floating (AC)
Is outside catering allowed? Can we bring our own caterer? +

Outside catering is not permitted. All food must be arranged through Samarambh's in-house kitchen, which allows us to maintain consistent quality and hygiene across every event.

  • Outside caterers not permitted
  • Veg menus available
  • Non-Veg menus available
  • Per-plate pricing varies by event & date
Is outside alcohol permitted? Do you have a bar? +

Yes — outside licensed alcohol is welcome at Samarambh. The venue does not stock or supply alcohol directly, but you are free to bring your own, provided it is legally licensed.

  • Alcohol not stocked at venue
  • Licensed outside alcohol permitted
  • Liquor licence arranged by venue — ₹20,000
What are the venue timings? Are late-night events allowed? +

Samarambh operates on two daily slots, allowing you to book either a daytime or an evening celebration.

  • Lunch slot — 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM
  • Dinner slot — 6:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight)
Is a DJ or live music allowed? What AV is available? +

Yes, music and entertainment are very much a part of the Samarambh experience. The venue provides a basic sound system and stage as part of the setup, along with full power backup.

  • Stage & sound system included
  • Full power backup
  • Homa / fire pooja permitted
  • DJ from ₹8,000
  • Projector & mic — additional charges
Is parking available? How do guests get to the venue? +

Samarambh offers dedicated on-site parking with a professional valet service available on event days. The venue sits directly on Ghodbunder Road, Thane West.

  • Parking for 75 vehicles
  • Valet parking available
  • On Ghodbunder Road, Thane West
  • Thane Bus Stop & Metro nearby
  • ~56 km from Mumbai Airport
Do you have guest rooms or accommodation on-site? +

Yes. Samarambh has 13 guest rooms on the premises — a meaningful convenience for out-of-town family and the bridal or groom's party who need to be nearby before or after the event.

  • 13 guest rooms on-site
  • Availability based on event date
Can I use my own decorator? Is décor included? +

You have full flexibility with décor. Samarambh has an in-house décor team experienced in thematic setups, floral mandaps, and contemporary arrangements.

  • In-house décor team available
  • Outside decorators permitted
  • Complimentary gift for banquet clients (event-dependent)
What types of events does Samarambh host — only weddings? +

While weddings are at the heart of what we do, Samarambh has over a decade of experience hosting the full range of life's milestones.

  • Weddings & Receptions
  • Engagement & Sangeet
  • Mehendi & Haldi
  • Birthday Parties
  • Baby Showers & Anniversaries
  • Corporate Events & Conferences
How do I book? What is the advance payment and cancellation policy? +

Begin by reaching out via phone, WhatsApp, or the inquiry form to confirm your event date and availability. Once details are agreed, a 50% advance secures your booking.

  • 50% advance confirms booking
  • Balance due 15 days before event
  • Non-cancellable & non-refundable
  • Standard GST applicable
